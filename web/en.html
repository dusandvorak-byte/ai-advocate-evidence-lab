CANNAINSIDER NEWS · 22 JULY 2026 · FRANZ KAFKA TODAY · REPORT 22072026-002

Alliance asks Prague court: Show the sample, method and measurement uncertainty for every cannabis trace

Cannabis is The Cure submitted a request to supplement evidence to the Prague Municipal Court in case 45 T 1/2024. It follows the Prague High Court decision of 29 July 2025, ref. 11 To 88/2024-2990, which quashed the earlier judgment in the relevant scope and returned the case for a new hearing.

9/9EXTREME RELEVANCE · ON FIRE

What the alliance requests: For every cannabis trace, identify its origin, form and weight, sampling method, representativeness, analytical method, distinction between THC and THCA, measurement uncertainty and the basis for every extrapolation.

Why it matters: The High Court criticised replacing expert questions with lay reasoning and stated that an expert result must not merely be adopted but critically checked and evaluated.

What the filing does not claim: The alliance is not a party to the criminal proceedings, does not represent the defendants, and the filing itself proves neither a laboratory error nor anyone's guilt or innocence. The court decides which evidence to take and the outcome.

Next monitored step: Publication of the exact date, courtroom and expected scope of the new public trial, previously reported as planned for October 2026.

Full source and evidence boundary

Cannabis is The Cure, z. s., filing to the Prague Municipal Court dated 22 July 2026, case 45 T 1/2024. Neither the filing nor its traffic-light score determines the outcome of evidence-taking.

Truth as the beginning of reconciliation, not a new instrument of retaliation

The idea was inspired by former South African ambassador Dr Noel Noa Lehoko speaking at a Friends of Africa seminar in Olomouc.
