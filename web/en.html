What an evidence-linked legal memory can do with records from 2004-2026

Legal analyses of CannaInsider.eu materials from 2004-2026, prepared between 20 April and 20 July 2026, produced a set of legal propositions useful when reviewing cases of people convicted or prosecuted in connection with cannabis. The sourced information was added to this website's evidence memory.

Since 20 July 2026, the system's usefulness has been demonstrated through specific anonymised submissions from the website creator's proceedings and those of five other people criminalised in connection with cannabis cultivation.

The system shows how relevant supplied material may be to a motion to reopen and separates a person's own facts from shared evidence and case law.

Privacy before adding a document

We recommend anonymising the document first. The prototype processes it locally in your browser and does not send it to a server, but automatic anonymisation may not detect every personal detail.

Evidence boundary: the traffic light measures relevance and the need for human review. It does not determine guilt, guarantee reopening, replace a lawyer, or transfer one case's conclusion to another.
